Welcome to Refrigerated Transport Service

RTS has been servicing the Kitchener-Waterloo region and surrounding areas as a trusted, family-owned repair shop specializing in refrigerated trailer (reefer) units. Conveniently located on Strasburg Road, our shop offers easy access for trucks and trailers of every size — with plenty of yard space to maneuver large rigs and multiple bays to get your equipment in and out quickly.

We understand that downtime costs you money, which is why we offer both in-shop service and mobile repair to keep your fleet on the road. From refrigeration unit diagnostics and repair, to trailer brakes, lighting, and structural fixes, to genuine parts and accessories — our experienced team handles it all. Whether you're an owner-operator or managing a multi-truck fleet, you can count on RTS for honest work, fair pricing, and the kind of service that has earned us a loyal customer base for years…
