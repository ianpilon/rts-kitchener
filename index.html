Why Choose Refrigerated Transport Service (RTS)

Reefer specialists serving Kitchener-Waterloo. Built around the realities of 42 yrs of hands on experience in cold-chain transport services.

Less Downtime, More Loads

Multiple bays, parts in stock, and fast diagnostics get your rigs back on the road quickly — because every hour a reefer sits is freight at risk.

Mobile Service Across KW

Can't make it to the shop? We come to your yard or roadside across Kitchener-Waterloo so a broken unit doesn't ground your route.

Reefer Specialists, Not Generalists

Decades on Thermo King and Carrier Transicold systems. Right diagnosis the first time — no parts cannon, no guessing on your dime.

What We Service

From rooftop reefer units to trailer suspensions, our team handles the full repair stack so your fleet stays cold and on the road.

  • Reefer Diagnostics & Repair Thermo King and Carrier Transicold systems — refrigerant, controls, defrost cycles, electrical.
  • Trailer Service Brakes, lighting, suspension, doors, and structural repairs. DOT-inspection ready.
  • Mobile On-Site Repair We come to your yard or roadside across Kitchener-Waterloo when you can't make it to ours.
  • Parts & Accessories Genuine OEM and quality aftermarket parts in stock for same-day turnaround.
