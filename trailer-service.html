Trailer Service

Reefer Repair

A reefer breakdown on the road can mean spoiled freight and serious losses. We service Thermo King and Carrier Transicold systems — refrigerant levels, defrost cycles, control-board issues, electrical, and routine preventive maintenance — so your unit keeps running cold and reliable through every season.

Trailer Repair

Brakes, lighting, suspension, doors, and structural repairs. We keep your trailers DOT-inspection ready and back on the road quickly — from routine maintenance to emergency fixes, every trailer in your fleet gets the same careful work.

Mobile Service & Fleet Support

Can't make it to the shop? Make RTS your first stop for on-site reefer and trailer repair. Our mobile crew comes to your yard or roadside across Ontario to get your equipment back on the road as quickly as possible — minimizing downtime and protecting your loads.
